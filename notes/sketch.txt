-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
S3 as long term storage
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Collectors record with this pattern to eventstore


bitstamp-collector-v1/bitstamp/btc-usd/trades/
bitstamp-collector-v2/bitstamp/btc-usd/orders/


Eventstore will automatically rollup those to daily snapshots, and upload them to permanent s3 storage


bitstamp-collector-v1/bitstamp/btc-usd/trades/13-04-2015
bitstamp-collector-v2/bitstamp/btc-usd/orders/13-04-2015-part // means not full day was recorded


storing historical data
storing buckets...
